Cookie Policy


  1. This Cookie Policy explains how Advarto Trading Limited ("we" or "us") uses cookies on the fitgym.pro website ("the site"). By using the site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.


1.What Are Cookies?


  1. 1.1. Cookies are small files that are downloaded to your computer or device when you visit a website. They serve various purposes, such as improving your browsing experience and providing information to the website owners. Cookies can store and retrieve information about your preferences, activities, and interactions on the site.


1.What Are Cookies?


  1. 2.1. We use cookies for the following reasons:


    2. 2.1.1. Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the site to function properly. They enable core functionality, such as accessing secure areas, managing the signup process, and processing orders. Disabling these cookies may disrupt the functionality and features of the site.


    2.1.2. Performance and Analytics Cookies: We use cookies, including those provided by Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use our site. These cookies help us analyse website traffic, track user interactions, and improve our content and services. The information collected is aggregated and anonymous, and it does not personally identify you.


    2.1.3. Preference Cookies: These cookies allow the site to remember your preferences and provide a personalized experience. They help us customize the site's appearance, language, and other settings according to your preferences.


    2.1.4. Advertising and Third-Party Cookies: We may use cookies provided by trusted third parties for advertising and marketing purposes. These cookies help us track the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns, measure referral sources, and credit affiliate partners appropriately.


3.Disabling Cookies


  1. 2.1. We use cookies for the following reasons:


    2. 3.1. You have the option to disable cookies by adjusting the settings in your browser. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality and features of the site. We recommend keeping cookies enabled to ensure a smooth and optimized browsing experience. If you are unsure whether you need certain cookies, it is generally safer to leave them enabled.


    3.2. For more information on managing cookie settings, refer to your browser's help documentation.


4.Third-Party Cookies


  1. 4.1. On occasion, we may use cookies provided by trusted third parties. These cookies serve various purposes, such as website analytics, testing new features, and tracking referral sources. The following section provides details on the specific third-party cookies you might encounter on our site.


    2. 4.2. Google Analytics:


    4.2.1. We utilize Google Analytics, a widely used web analytics service provided by Google. Google Analytics cookies help us understand how users interact with our site, including the pages visited and the time spent on each page.


    4.2.2. This information enables us to improve the site's content and user experience.


    4.2.3. For more information on Google Analytics cookies, please visit the official Google Analytics page.
